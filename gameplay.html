Gameplay Mechanics

Tower defense with cultural depth and strategic complexity

Core Gameplay

Sakartvelo Defenders combines the classic tower defense mechanics with historical accuracy and cultural immersion. Players must strategically place defensive structures to defend against waves of enemies representing different historical periods.

Tower Types

Each historical era has its own specialized tower types that reflect the technological and cultural capabilities of that period:

Ancient Defenses (1500 BC - 337 AD)

  • Stone Walls
  • Battering Rams
  • Wooden Towers
  • Slingshots

Medieval Fortress (337 - 1466)

  • Stone Castles
  • Ballista
  • Mounted Archers
  • Fire Arrows

Golden Age (1466 - 1502)

  • Gunpowder Towers
  • Fortifications
  • Arquebusiers
  • Cannon

Modern Era (1502 - present)

  • Modern Artillery
  • Aircraft Defenses
  • Advanced Radar
  • Anti-Tank Weapons

Enemy Types

Enemies in each era reflect the military tactics and technology of that period:

Early Warriors

Basic infantry with spears and shields from ancient times.

Medieval Knights

Fully armored cavalry from the medieval period.

Gunpowder Soldiers

Infantry with rifles and cannon from the modern period.

Strategic Elements

Gameplay involves complex decisions about resource management and strategic placement:

  • Resource allocation between different tower types
  • Terrain-based defenses (mountains, rivers, cities)
  • Era-specific upgrades that reflect historical advancement
  • Skill-based progression and unlocking of advanced units

Progression System

Players progress through different historical eras, with each era representing a new level of complexity in both gameplay and historical content. The progression system is based on gameplay skill rather than monetary investment.

Historical Accuracy

Each era's gameplay mechanics, enemy types, and tower capabilities are researched and designed to reflect real historical periods. This isn't just a "history as skin" game - the historical period directly influences gameplay.

Multiplayer Features

Future multiplayer features are planned to allow players to collaborate or compete in defending historical battles from different periods.
