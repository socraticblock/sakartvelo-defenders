About Sakartvelo Defenders

A cultural celebration through historical gameplay

Our Vision

Sakartvelo Defenders is more than just a game - it's a journey through 9,000 years of Georgian history. We set out to create a tower defense game that truly respects and celebrates Georgia's rich cultural heritage while incorporating modern game design principles and blockchain technology.

The Idea

The concept emerged from a desire to preserve and share Georgian history with younger generations. Tower defense games are popular, but there's a lack of historical context and cultural authenticity in most titles. Our mission was to create a game that would teach players about Georgian history while providing the fun, engaging gameplay they expect.

Developer Background

Built by a solo developer passionate about both gaming and Georgian culture, Sakartvelo Defenders represents a personal project with a global impact. The developer has spent countless hours researching historical battles, cultural practices, and military strategies from each era of Georgian history.

Why This Approach

Traditional historical games often fall into two categories: overly simplified or too academic to be engaging. Sakartvelo Defenders aims for the sweet spot where history is authentically represented, but in a fun, accessible way that anyone can enjoy.

Educational Value

Each era in the game is designed to educate players about real historical events, strategies, and cultural practices while providing engaging gameplay that makes learning memorable and fun.

Community Focus

We're building a community of history enthusiasts and gamers passionate about Georgian culture. Early access participants will be involved in development, with their feedback helping to shape the final experience.

Sustainability

Sakartvelo Defenders is designed to be sustainable with minimal resources. The game can run on modest servers, with blockchain only being used for token economy, not game state storage.
