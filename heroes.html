Active Abilities

Three Abilities. Total Control.

Each hero has three active abilities on independent cooldowns. Basic abilities are ready frequently -- ultimates require timing and game sense. Heroes are era-locked: you can only deploy a hero in levels from their historical period.

●

Basic Ability

15-20s CD

Available frequently. Used to control immediate threats, boost economy, or set up synergy combos. The ability you use most in any given run.

●●

Advanced Ability

45-60s CD

Higher impact, longer cooldown. Often shapes the flow of a wave or provides critical defensive utility. Requires more deliberate timing.

★

Ultimate Ability

120-180s CD

Map-wide or boss-eliminating power. Saving your ultimate for the right moment -- a boss phase transition, a wave 20 swarm -- is the difference between 2 stars and 3.

Hero Progression

Level Up Through Battle

▲

XP Gain

Every battle grants hero XP proportional to waves survived and enemies defeated. Replay any level -- XP is earned on every attempt, not just first completion. No daily limits, no energy systems.

⚡

Level Benefits

Each level improves two random hero stats (damage, cooldown reduction, duration, area) and reduces all ability cooldowns by 1%. Max level is capped to prevent power inflation across the player base.
