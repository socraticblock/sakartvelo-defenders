# Sakartvelo Defenders — Tower Synergy Reference

> Synergies activate when towers are placed within 3-tile adjacency of each other.
> Each tower can contribute to multiple synergies simultaneously.
> Synergy bonuses stack additively unless noted otherwise.

---

## Two-Tower Synergies (28)

### Era 0 — Foundations of Kartli

**1. Khevsur Volley**
- Combination: Archer Tower + Archer Tower
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +15% attack speed for both
- Flavor: The highland warriors of Khevsureti were famed for their coordinated archery volleys. Two archer towers working in rhythm unleash a relentless rain of arrows.

**2. Kartli Ambush**
- Combination: Archer Tower + Catapult
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +20% damage for both; catapult shots deal bonus damage to enemies marked by archers
- Flavor: Inspired by the hit-and-run tactics of ancient Iberian kingdoms, archers flush enemies into the path of devastating stone bombardments.

**3. Tbilisi Ramparts**
- Combination: Archer Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +25% range for archers; archers placed adjacent to walls gain elevated targeting
- Flavor: Since the founding of Tbilisi by King Vakhtang Gorgasali, elevated rampart positions have given Georgian defenders unmatched line of sight over attackers.

**4. Narikala Bombardment**
- Combination: Catapult + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +20% splash radius for catapults; redirected enemies take 10% more damage
- Flavor: The ancient fortress of Narikala overlooking the Mtkvari River funneled invaders into devastating kill zones — a strategy as old as the citadel itself.

**5. Mtkvari Crossing**
- Combination: Wall/Barrier + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +50% wall HP; enemies redirected between walls take 5% damage per redirect
- Flavor: Controlling the fords of the Mtkvari River was the key to holding the Georgian heartland. Layered barriers turned crossings into death traps.

### Era 1 — The Golden Age Dawns

**6. Didgori Formation**
- Combination: Archer Tower + Cavalry Outpost
- Introduced: Era 1
- Bonus: +20% attack speed for both towers
- Flavor: At the Battle of Didgori in 1121, King David the Builder's combined arms of archers and cavalry shattered a Seljuk army five times their size. Their coordination remains legendary.

**7. Dariali Gate Guard**
- Combination: Cavalry Outpost + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 1
- Bonus: +30% enemy intercept range for cavalry; cavalry near walls deal +15% bonus damage
- Flavor: The Dariali Pass in the Caucasus was Georgia's northern gateway. Cavalry stationed at fortified chokepoints could intercept any incursion before it reached the lowlands.

**8. Georgian Shieldwall**
- Combination: Cavalry Outpost + Catapult
- Introduced: Era 1
- Bonus: Cavalry attacks slow enemies by 20% for 2 seconds; catapults deal +15% damage to slowed enemies
- Flavor: King David's heavy cavalry would pin enemies in place with disciplined charges, allowing his siege engines to devastate the immobilized formations.

### Era 2 — Age of Fortresses

**9. Rabati Garrison**
- Combination: Siege Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 2
- Bonus: +20% armor (damage reduction) for both towers; walls adjacent to siege towers reflect 10% of melee damage
- Flavor: The great fortress of Rabati in Akhaltsikhe was repeatedly rebuilt and reinforced across centuries, its garrisons turning it into an impregnable symbol of Georgian resilience.

**10. Shamkhor Storm**
- Combination: Siege Tower + Catapult
- Introduced: Era 2
- Bonus: +25% damage for both; catapult and siege attacks create minor shockwaves that briefly stun nearby enemies
- Flavor: After the liberation of Shamkhor from the Seljuks in 1195, Georgian siege warfare reached new heights of coordination between heavy engines.

**11. Katskhi Pillar**
- Combination: Siege Tower + Archer Tower
- Introduced: Era 2
- Bonus: +30% range for archers; +15% damage for siege tower; archers can fire over walls
- Flavor: The Katskhi Pillar — a 40-meter limestone monolith topped by a church — symbolizes how elevation and steadfastness multiply defensive power.

**12. Alazani Charge**
- Combination: Cavalry Outpost + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 2
- Bonus: Cavalry charges stun enemies for 1 second; siege towers deal +35% damage to stunned enemies
- Flavor: In the Battle of Shamkhor (1195), Queen Tamar's cavalry charges shattered enemy lines, exposing them to devastating follow-up attacks from siege positions.

### Era 3 — Sacred Wardens

**13. Mtskheta Blessing**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Archer Tower
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Archers gain +20% damage; shrine towers regenerate mana 15% faster when near archers
- Flavor: Mtskheta, the ancient capital and spiritual heart of Georgia, was where Christianity first took root. Sacred blessing amplified the strength of every defender.

**14. Vardzia Echo**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Catapult
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Catapult impact zones linger as 3-second magic damage fields; +10% splash damage
- Flavor: The cave city of Vardzia, carved into the cliffsides by Queen Tamar, held secrets of ancient magic that made the earth itself rise against invaders.

**15. Gergeti Ward**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Walls emit a slow aura (15% reduction) in a 2-tile radius; magic shrine gains +20% mana regeneration
- Flavor: The Church of the Holy Trinity at Gergeti, standing watch over Stepantsminda, radiates a protective presence that slows all who approach with ill intent.

**16. Tamar's Grace**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Cavalry Outpost
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Cavalry attacks have a 15% chance to heal all adjacent towers for 2% of their max HP
- Flavor: Queen Tamar was revered as a holy ruler. Her cavalry carried not only swords but the blessings of the Georgian Church, sustaining defenders wherever they rode.

**17. Svaneti Ward**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Siege tower attacks apply a "Weaken" debuff — affected enemies take 20% more damage from all sources for 3 seconds
- Flavor: The fierce towers of Svaneti, standing sentinel over mountain valleys for a millennium, were said to carry protective enchantments that broke the strength of any who approached.

**18. Gelati Academy**
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Magic/Shrine Tower
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: +25% buff duration and -15% debuff effectiveness on allied towers; shrine aura range increased by 1 tile
- Flavor: King David the Builder founded the Gelati Academy as a center of learning and faith. Two shrines working in harmony create a circle of knowledge that protects all within.

### Era 5 — Thunder of Gunpowder

**19. Gori Cannonade**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Archer Tower
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: Archers mark high-value targets; gunpowder towers deal +30% damage to marked targets
- Flavor: The garrison town of Gori, birthplace of a titan and a military stronghold, pioneered combined-arms tactics where traditional archery guided the devastating accuracy of cannon fire.

**20. Tbilisi Arsenal**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Catapult
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: +20% attack speed for both; catapult projectiles gain a 15% chance to ignite enemies for 3 seconds
- Flavor: The expansion of the Tbilisi Arsenal under King Erekle II brought medieval siege engines and modern cannon together, doubling the city's defensive output.

**21. Khertvisi Stronghold**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: Walls gain "Thorns" — enemies that attack or pass adjacent walls take 8% of their max HP as damage
- Flavor: The impregnable fortress of Khertvisi at the confluence of the Mtkvari and Paravani rivers punished any force foolish enough to assault its walls.

**22. Telavi Vanguard**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Cavalry Outpost
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: Cavalry herds scatter enemies into tighter formations; gunpowder towers gain +20% splash radius against grouped enemies
- Flavor: King Erekle II's reforms at Telavi modernized Georgian cavalry tactics, teaching riders to drive enemies into the kill zones of newly acquired artillery.

**23. Dmanisi Convergence**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Magic/Shrine Tower
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: Enemies killed by gunpowder towers explode for 15% of their max HP as magic damage to nearby foes
- Flavor: Dmanisi, where the earliest human ancestors were found outside Africa, represents the convergence of old and new — ancient magic and modern firepower united.

**24. Kutaisi Forge**
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: +25% damage for both; gunpowder projectiles gain slight splash; siege attacks deal bonus damage to fortified enemies
- Flavor: Kutaisi, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, housed forges where the weapons of every era were refined and combined into instruments of war.

---

### Era 6 — Industrial Revolution

**25. Chiatura Mines**
- Combination: Industrial Tower + Gunpowder Tower
- Introduced: Era 6
- Bonus: +30% attack speed for both; gunpowder towers consume 10% less ammunition (if applicable)
- Flavor: The manganese mines of Chiatura fueled Georgia's industrial ambitions. The raw materials flowing from its depths powered both industry and arms.

**26. Rustavi Foundry**
- Combination: Industrial Tower + Magic/Shrine Tower
- Introduced: Era 6
- Bonus: Industrial towers gain a magic shield absorbing 15% of incoming damage; shrine towers get +20% buff potency on industrial towers
- Flavor: Rustavi's massive steel works became a symbol of Georgian industry. When combined with ancient spiritual power, even machines gained supernatural resilience.

**27. Surami Tunnel**
- Combination: Industrial Tower + Cavalry Outpost
- Introduced: Era 6
- Bonus: Cavalry gains +25% movement speed and can pass through walls; industrial towers near cavalry produce +10% faster
- Flavor: The Surami Tunnel through the Likhi Range connected eastern and western Georgia. Industrial-era cavalry used new routes to outflank and outmaneuver any defense.

**28. Batumi Port**
- Combination: Industrial Tower + Bunker Tower
- Introduced: Era 6 (prereq for Bunker in Era 8)
- Bonus: +20% range for both; bunker repairs itself 5% faster per adjacent industrial tower
- Flavor: The Black Sea port of Batumi became a hub of trade and military logistics. Supply lines from the port kept defensive positions operational far beyond normal capacity.

---

### Era 8 — Fortress Era

**29. Gori Fortress Line**
- Combination: Bunker Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 8
- Bonus: +40% HP for both; enemies attacking walls take 12% reflected damage; bunker deals 10% more damage to enemies near walls
- Flavor: The modernized fortress lines around Gori represented the pinnacle of Georgian defensive engineering — walls and bunkers working as a single organism.

**30. Kobuleti Defense Line**
- Combination: Bunker Tower + Gunpowder Tower
- Introduced: Era 8
- Bonus: +25% damage for both; bunker absorbs friendly fire splash damage, protecting adjacent gunpowder towers from their own blasts
- Flavor: The coastal defense line at Kobuleti, guarding the Black Sea approach, demonstrated how armored bunkers could safely support devastating artillery positions.

---

### Era 9 — Technological Ascendancy

**31. Mziuri Circuit**
- Combination: Tech Tower + Gunpowder Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: Gunpowder projectiles become guided (+15% accuracy vs. moving targets); tech tower gains +20% range
- Flavor: The children's park of Mziuri in Tbilisi, with its futuristic design, represents Georgia's embrace of technology. Modern targeting systems made even traditional weapons devastating.

**32. Poti Terminal**
- Combination: Tech Tower + Industrial Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: +25% attack speed for both; tech tower deploys repair drones that also boost industrial production by 10%
- Flavor: The modernized Port of Poti on the Black Sea became a node of technological and industrial integration, where automation and heavy industry reinforced each other.

**33. Gudauri Signal**
- Combination: Tech Tower + Magic/Shrine Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: Shrine debuffs spread to 1 additional nearby enemy; tech tower gains +15% precision (critical hit chance)
- Flavor: The mountain resort of Gudauri, high in the Caucasus, became a communications hub where ancient signals and modern frequencies merged into something greater.

**34. Tbilisi Metro**
- Combination: Tech Tower + Bunker Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: Bunkers gain the ability to relocate once every 30 seconds; tech tower drones provide +10% armor to all adjacent towers
- Flavor: The Tbilisi Metro, renowned for its depth and ornate stations, symbolizes underground resilience. Modern bunkers can shift positions, keeping enemies永远 guessing.

**35. Kvesheti Scope**
- Combination: Tech Tower + Archer Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: Archers gain +40% range and +10% critical hit chance; tech tower processes archer targeting data for +15% accuracy across all adjacent towers
- Flavor: The Kvesheti Tunnel project demonstrated precision engineering in the Georgian mountains. When ancient archery meets modern targeting optics, no enemy is safe.

## Three-Tower Synergies (10)

### 1. Battle of Didgori
- Combination: Archer Tower + Cavalry Outpost + Magic/Shrine Tower
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: All three gain +25% damage and +15% attack speed; enemies within 3 tiles of any tower in the trio are weakened (take 10% more damage from all sources)
- Flavor: "The Miraculous Victory" of 1121. King David the Builder united archers, cavalry, and the blessing of the Georgian Church to defeat the Seljuk coalition. The ground itself still remembers that holy alliance.

### 2. Narikala Siege Complex
- Combination: Wall/Barrier + Catapult + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 2
- Bonus: Walls redirect enemies into optimal catapult/siege firing lanes; +30% splash radius for catapult; siege tower deals +20% damage to enemies adjacent to walls; walls gain +25% HP
- Flavor: The layered defenses of Narikala Fortress — walls channeling invaders into overlapping fields of catapult and siege fire — made the citadel nearly unconquerable for over 1,500 years.

### 3. Svetitskhoveli Sanctuary
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Archer Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Sacred ground aura: all enemies within range are slowed by 20%; archers gain +30% damage; walls regenerate 3% HP per second; shrine mana regeneration doubled
- Flavor: The Svetitskhoveli Cathedral in Mtskheta, believed to house the robe of Christ, created a sanctuary where evil could not thrive. Defenders within its shadow fought with divine endurance.

### 4. Kartli Foundation
- Combination: Archer Tower + Catapult + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 0
- Bonus: +15% to all stats (damage, attack speed, range, armor) for all three towers; enemies redirected by walls take +10% damage from archers and catapults; walls redirect enemies more aggressively
- Flavor: The three pillars of ancient Iberian defense — walls to channel, archers to weaken, and engines to crush. This foundational combo rewards players who master the basics and carry them forward through every era.

### 5. Kutaisi Bombardment
- Combination: Catapult + Gunpowder Tower + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 5
- Bonus: Staggered barrage pattern — attacks from all three never overlap, ensuring continuous fire; +35% damage for each; kills by any tower in the trio create minor shockwaves that stun nearby enemies for 0.5 seconds
- Flavor: Kutaisi under siege was a symphony of destruction. Ancient trebuchets, modern cannon, and heavy siege engines fired in devastating rotation, leaving no moment of respite for attackers.

### 6. Tamar's Iron Ring
- Combination: Cavalry Outpost + Magic/Shrine Tower + Wall/Barrier
- Introduced: Era 3
- Bonus: Cavalry patrols in a wider radius around walls; shrine creates a healing aura that restores 2% HP per second to all adjacent towers and cavalry; walls emit a fear aura that reduces enemy movement speed by 25%
- Flavor: Queen Tamar's defensive strategy created an "iron ring" around the Georgian kingdom — cavalry screening the approaches, sacred shrines sustaining the defenders, and walls holding the line. No army broke that ring during her reign.

### 7. Soviet Defense Line
- Combination: Gunpowder Tower + Industrial Tower + Bunker Tower
- Introduced: Era 8
- Bonus: Interlocking fields of fire — +25% damage for all three; suppression aura slows enemies by 15% in a 4-tile radius; bunker gains +30% armor; industrial tower repairs all three at 2% HP per second
- Flavor: The industrial-era defense doctrines that hardened Georgia's borders created kill zones of extraordinary lethality. Bunkers, artillery, and assembly-line production formed a trinity of modern warfare.

### 8. Rustavi War Machine
- Combination: Magic/Shrine Tower + Industrial Tower + Siege Tower
- Introduced: Era 6
- Bonus: Industrial towers repair siege engines and shrines at 3% HP per second; siege attacks gain +30% damage and apply a magic-based corrosion debuff (enemies lose 5% armor per hit, stacking 3 times); shrine buffs extend to siege tower
- Flavor: Rustavi's industrial might, powered by Georgian ingenuity and ancient mystical knowledge, produced siege engines of terrifying power — machines that carried both the weight of steel and the force of old magic.

### 9. Alazani Mechanized Division
- Combination: Cavalry Outpost + Tech Tower + Gunpowder Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: Drone-cavalry coordination — tech drones mark targets for cavalry intercepts (+25% cavalry damage to marked targets); gunpowder towers gain +20% attack speed; cavalry movement speed +30%; marked targets take +15% damage from all sources
- Flavor: Along the Alazani Valley, where ancient cavalry charges once decided the fate of kingdoms, modern technology has created a mechanized cavalry concept — drones spotting, riders striking, and artillery finishing the job.

### 10. Gudauri Nexus
- Combination: Tech Tower + Magic/Shrine Tower + Bunker Tower
- Introduced: Era 9
- Bonus: EMP-Magic hybrid pulses every 8 seconds disable enemy abilities and apply a silence debuff for 2 seconds in a 3-tile radius; bunker gains a magic shield absorbing 20% of damage; tech tower drones auto-repair the bunker; shrine regenerates 50% faster
- Flavor: High in the Caucasus at Gudauri, a secret nexus of technology and ancient power was said to exist. Here, the barriers between science and magic dissolved — creating a defensive network that could shield, strike, and sustain simultaneously.

## Synergy Activation Rules

1. All two-tower synergies activate when both towers are within 3 tiles of each other (Manhattan distance).
2. Three-tower synergies require ALL three towers to be within 3 tiles of at least one other tower in the trio (connected cluster).
3. A single tower can participate in multiple synergies simultaneously. For example, a Wall adjacent to both an Archer and a Cavalry activates both "Tbilisi Ramparts" and "Dariali Gate Guard."
4. Synergy bonuses are additive. If two synergies grant +15% attack speed, the tower receives +30% total.
5. Three-tower synergies override any conflicting two-tower synergy bonuses — the three-tower bonus replaces (does not stack with) the two-tower bonuses among the same three towers.
6. Era-gated synergies only activate once the player has reached the specified era, even if the towers are placed in advance.
