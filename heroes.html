Georgian Legends

Heroes of History

Command legendary figures from across Georgia's 3,000-year history. Each hero brings unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

Era 0 — Ancient Colchis

Medea

  • Toxic Cloud
    Creates a lingering poison cloud that damages enemies over time.
  • Healing Rain
    Restores health to all friendly units in a large area.
  • Golden Fleece Shield
    Grants temporary invulnerability to towers within range.
Era 1 — Kingdom of Iberia

Pharnavaz I

  • Royal Authority
    Increases attack speed of all towers by 20% for 10 seconds.
  • King's Guard
    Summons elite soldiers to defend a choke point.
Era 1 — Kingdom of Iberia

Saint Nino

  • Divine Light
    Reveals all invisible enemies and traps in a wide radius.
  • Blessing of Peace
    Stuns all enemies in an area for 3 seconds.
  • Cross of Vine
    Creates barriers that slow enemies by 50%.
Era 1 — Kingdom of Iberia

Vakhtang Gorgasali

  • Wolf Head Strike
    Deals massive damage to a single target, ignoring armor.
  • Founder's Will
    Permanently increases the range of all towers on the map.
Era 3 — Georgian Golden Age

David IV the Builder

  • Instant Upgrade
    Instantly upgrades all towers one level at no cost.
  • Didgori Victory
    All towers deal 50% more damage for 15 seconds.
  • Stone Wall
    Creates an indestructible wall segment.
Era 3 — Georgian Golden Age

Queen Tamar

  • Global Damage Boost
    All towers deal 30% more damage for 20 seconds.
  • Queen's Decree
    Reduces enemy movement speed by 40% across the entire map.
  • Golden Age Glory
    Passive: Generates extra gold from all enemy kills.
Era 3 — Georgian Golden Age

Shota Rustaveli

  • Knight in Panther's Skin
    Summons a powerful knight that fights alongside towers.
  • Poetic Justice
    Reflects 50% of damage taken back to attacking enemies.
Era 5 — Between Empires

King Erekle II

  • Gunpowder Barrage
    Fires cannons in a line, damaging all enemies.
  • Strategic Defense
    Increases tower armor and resistance by 50%.
Era 6 — Russian Empire

Ilia Chavchavadze

  • National Awakening
    Inspires towers to attack 40% faster.
  • Cultural Shield
    Creates a barrier that protects towers from special attacks.
Era 7 — First Democratic Republic

Noe Jordania

  • Democratic Defense
    All towers share damage, preventing any from being overwhelmed.
  • Independence Rally
    Boosts all tower stats by 25% for the remainder of the wave.
Era 8 — The Soviet Century

Unknown Soldier

  • Artillery Strike
    Calls in a devastating artillery barrage on target area.
  • Motherland's Call
    Temporarily doubles all tower attack power.
  • Survival Instinct
    Towers continue attacking at reduced efficiency even when destroyed.
Era 9 — Modern Georgia

Modern Defender

  • Drone Strike
    Deploy drones that attack multiple targets simultaneously.
  • Precision Targeting
    All towers gain 100% critical hit chance for 10 seconds.
  • Technological Superiority
    Passive: Towers slowly regenerate health over time.
