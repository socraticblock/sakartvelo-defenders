Core Design Principles

Built Different. Built Right.

☆

Invisible Blockchain

Players log in with email -- wallet is created automatically. No MetaMask tutorials. No seed phrases. The blockchain is infrastructure, not a feature. Just play.

★

Skill > Wallet

Every gameplay-affecting item is earned through skill -- towers, heroes, scrolls, and skins. A player who spends $0 has the exact same experience as someone who spends $1,000.

☠

Historical Accuracy

Chronologically accurate. When the historical record is ambiguous, the game acknowledges uncertainty rather than presenting speculation as fact. Tower types, enemy formations, hero abilities, and map designs are all drawn from real Georgian history.

⚔

No-Nerf Rule

Once released, core functionality is never nerfed. If a strategy is too strong, we buff everything else to match -- adding counter-strategies rather than removing player agency. Players who master a system keep their mastery.

⚙

Accessibility

Must be accessible to players with no prior experience with tower defense, blockchain, or Georgian history. The tutorial teaches core mechanics organically -- no walls of text, no prerequisite knowledge.

Ten Eras of Georgian History

3,000 Years. One Epic Journey.

Each era introduces unique towers, enemies, heroes, and mechanics. Eras are unlocked sequentially -- complete 15 of 20 levels in the current era to proceed.

Era 0 / Tutorial

Ancient Colchis

~600 BC - ~100 BC

The legendary kingdom of the Golden Fleece. The Medea statue in Batumi overlooks the Black Sea coast. Explore the archaeological site of Vani, gold-panning with sheep fleeces, and battle devi -- many-headed ogres from Georgian myth. New player onboarding with simpler mechanics.

Era 1

Kingdom of Iberia

~300 BC - 630 AD

Pharnavaz, St. Nino, Vakhtang Gorgasali. Roman-Parthian rivalry, Christianization of Georgia. Introduces hero abilities and synergy mechanics.

Era 2

Age of Invasions

~630 - 1089 AD

Arab Emirs, Bagrat III the Unifier, Didi Turkoba (Great Turkish Onslaught). Fortified sieges against Arab and Seljuk forces. Advanced synergy chains in snow-capped fortresses.

Era 3

The Georgian Golden Age

~1089 - 1225 AD

David IV, Queen Tamar, Zakare Zakarian, Empire of Trebizond. The zenith of Georgian power and culture. Royal guard towers, cathedral cannons, battlefield engineering.

Era 4

The Mongol Catastrophe

~1225 - 1500 AD

Queen Rusudan, George V the Brilliant, General Chormaqan. Jalal ad-Din's devastation, then Mongol conquest. Timur's second invasion shatters the remnant. Mountain fortress survival. Introduces mastery scroll system.

Era 5

Between Empires

~1500 - 1801 AD

Vakhtang VI the Lawgiver, King Erekle II, Totleben betrayal at Aspindza (1770). Gunpowder appears. Caught between Ottoman and Persian empires.

Era 6

Russian Empire

~1801 - 1918 AD

Prince Pyotr Bagration, Cultural Figures. Industrial-age technology, cultural renaissance under occupation.

Era 7

First Democratic Republic

~1918 - 1921 AD

Noe Jordania, Transcaucasian Federation. Brief democracy between empires -- British military presence 1918-1920, League of Nations application postponed. Three years of sovereignty before Soviet invasion.

Era 8

The Soviet Century

~1921 - ~1991 AD

Soviet Era Figures. Soviet military tech, ~190,000 Georgian KIA in WWII, 1956 Tbilisi Massacre, independence movement. Introduces survival mode.

Era 9

Modern Georgia

~1991 - Present

Gamsakhurdia, Saakashvili, Modern Democratic Georgia. Precision weapons, drone platforms. 2008 war with Russian military (not separatists). 2023-2024 political crisis and frozen EU candidacy. Endgame content.

Core Game Loop

How It Works

Build Mazes

Place walls to create winding paths. Position towers for overlapping kill zones. Every wall segment and tower position matters -- a well-designed maze turns basic towers into a devastating defense.

Command Heroes

Directly control heroes from Georgian history and legend. Medea's toxic clouds, Queen Tamar's global damage boost, David the Builder's instant tower upgrades. Each hero has 2-3 active abilities with cooldowns.

Earn Stars

1-3 stars per level. First-time star unlocks earn SAKART tokens. Each star can only be earned once -- replay farming is mathematically impossible.

Unlock Eras

Complete 15 of 20 levels in the current era to unlock the next. No SAKART fee required -- progression is purely skill-based. Each era brings new tower types, enemies, heroes, maps, and mechanics.
