Core Design Principles

Built Different. Built Right.

⚔

Play First, Blockchain Second

The blockchain is invisible infrastructure. Players never need to understand crypto, wallets, or gas fees. They click "Play" and earn tokens naturally through skilled gameplay. No MetaMask tutorials. No seed phrases. Just play.

★

Skill > Wallet

Every gameplay-affecting item is earned through skill. A player who spends $0 has the exact same experience as someone who spends $1,000. SAKART tokens are earned by three-starring levels, not by swiping a credit card.

☠

History Drives Gameplay

This isn't "culture as skin." Tower types, enemy formations, hero abilities, and map designs are all drawn from real Georgian history. You're not just playing a tower defense -- you're defending actual historical battles.

Ten Eras of Georgian History

3,000 Years. One Epic Journey.

Each era introduces unique towers, enemies, heroes, and mechanics. Eras are unlocked sequentially -- complete 15 of 20 levels in the current era to proceed.

Era 0 / Tutorial

Ancient Colchis

~1500 BC - ~300 BC

The legendary kingdom of the Golden Fleece. Medea, Aeetes, Jason. Dense Colchian forests, the Golden Fleece. New player onboarding with simpler mechanics.

Era 1

Kingdom of Iberia

~300 BC - 630 AD

Pharnavaz, St. Nino, Vakhtang Gorgasali. Roman-Parthian rivalry, Christianization of Georgia. Introduces hero abilities and synergy mechanics.

Era 2

Age of Invasions

~630 - 1089 AD

Arab Emirs, Bagrationi Kings. Fortified sieges against Arab and Seljuk forces. Advanced synergy chains in snow-capped fortresses.

Era 3

The Georgian Golden Age

~1089 - 1225 AD

David IV, Queen Tamar. The zenith of Georgian power and culture. Royal guard towers, cathedral cannons, battlefield engineering.

Era 4

The Mongol Catastrophe

~1225 - 1500 AD

Mongol Generals, Fragmented Kings. Devastation and retreat to mountain fortresses. Introduces mastery scroll system.

Era 5

Between Empires

~1500 - 1801 AD

King Erekle II, Persian/Ottoman Rivals. Gunpowder appears. Caught between Ottoman and Persian empires.

Era 6

Russian Empire

~1801 - 1918 AD

Annexed Nobility, Cultural Figures. Industrial-age technology, cultural renaissance under occupation.

Era 7

First Democratic Republic

~1918 - 1921 AD

Noe Jordania, Independence Fighters. Brief democracy between empires. Shortest era.

Era 8

The Soviet Century

~1921 - ~1991 AD

Soviet Era Figures. Soviet military tech, WWII, independence movement. Introduces survival mode.

Era 9

Modern Georgia

~1991 - Present

Modern democratic Georgia. Precision weapons, drone platforms, 2008 war. Endgame content.

Core Game Loop

How It Works

Build Mazes

Place walls to create winding paths. Position towers for overlapping kill zones. Every wall segment and tower position matters -- a well-designed maze turns basic towers into a devastating defense.

Command Heroes

Directly control heroes from Georgian history and legend. Medea's toxic clouds, Queen Tamar's global damage boost, David the Builder's instant tower upgrades. Each hero has 2-3 active abilities with cooldowns.

Earn Stars

1-3 stars per level. First-time star unlocks earn SAKART tokens. Replays earn zero -- no grinding, no farming.

Unlock Eras

Complete 15 of 20 levels in the current era to unlock the next. No SAKART fee required -- progression is purely skill-based. Each era brings new tower types, enemies, heroes, maps, and mechanics.
