Player Experience

The Invisible Blockchain

Most blockchain games lead with crypto. We don't. The blockchain is silent infrastructure that players never interact with directly. Here's what the experience actually looks like.

✉

Log In With Email

Players sign up with an email address. Immutable Passport automatically creates and manages a wallet behind the scenes. No seed phrases, no browser extensions, no crypto tutorials. You click "Sign Up" and you're in.

★

Earn Through Gameplay

Three-star a level for the first time and SAKART tokens appear in your balance. No mining. No staking. No daily quests to grind. You play the game well, the game rewards you. Tokens show up like any in-game currency.

⚙

Spend on Cosmetics

Use SAKART to buy cosmetic tower skins, hero visual effects, and other vanity items. Gameplay-affecting items are never sold -- only earned. The marketplace lets players trade skins with each other, with a 1.8% fee sustaining development.

What Players See

  • SAKART token balance in the UI

Token Details

SAKART Tokenomics

SAKART is a utility token with a fixed maximum supply. It is earned exclusively through skilled gameplay -- never purchased, never mined, never staked for yield. Every token enters circulation because a player demonstrated skill.

Supply & Distribution

  • Max Supply: 500,000,000 SAKART (hard cap)
  • Emission: First-time star unlocks only
  • Grinding: Replaying levels earns zero tokens
  • Purchase: SAKART cannot be bought from the game
  • Deflationary: Tokens spent on fees are burned

Design Principles

  • No pay-to-earn: Spending money gives zero gameplay advantage
  • No grinding: Each star can only earn tokens once
  • Skill-gated: Better play = more tokens, period
  • Fixed supply: No inflation, no new tokens minted beyond the cap
  • Circulation: Tokens move between players via marketplace

Token Emission

Earning SAKART

There is exactly one way to earn SAKART tokens: demonstrate skill for the first time. No exceptions. No loopholes. No grind.

★

First-Time Star Unlocks

Each level awards 1-3 stars based on performance. When you earn a star for the first time, you receive SAKART tokens. The key word is first time -- replaying a level you've already starred earns nothing. No grinding. No farming. No botting.

⚔

Difficulty Multipliers

Stars are tracked per difficulty tier: Normal, Hard, and Elite. Earning your first star on Hard difficulty pays more than Normal. Elite pays even more. This rewards genuine skill improvement, not repetition. Each difficulty tier is a fresh earning opportunity.

▲

Era Completion Bonuses

Completing all levels in an era with full stars triggers a one-time bonus. These bonuses are larger than individual star rewards and serve as milestones that mark genuine mastery of an era's mechanics and strategies.

The Anti-Grind Guarantee

Every token reward in Sakartvelo Defenders is one-time-only. You cannot replay content for tokens. You cannot pay to earn. You cannot bot your way to wealth. SAKART enters circulation only when a player achieves something they haven't achieved before. This is non-negotiable.

Technical Architecture

Smart Contract Architecture

Four contracts. One chain. Zero gas fees. Built on Immutable zkEVM for Ethereum-grade security with a player-friendly experience.

◆

Token Contract (SAKART)

ERC-20 compatible utility token on Immutable zkEVM. Fixed max supply of 500M. Minting is governed by the game's anti-grind rules -- tokens are only created when a player earns a first-time star unlock. No admin minting, no reserve allocation.

ERC-20 Fixed Supply
◆

NFT Mint Contract

Mints cosmetic tower skins and hero visual effects as ERC-721 tokens. Each skin has metadata linking it to a specific Georgian historical art style or motif. Minting is triggered by in-game purchases using SAKART tokens. No external minting access.

ERC-721 Cosmetic Only
◆

Marketplace Contract

Handles listing, buying, and trading of cosmetic NFTs between players. Enforces the 1.8% fee on every sale. Funds are routed automatically -- 98.2% to the seller, 1.8% to the development wallet. No escrow delays, no manual processing.

1.8% Fee Peer-to-Peer

Built on Immutable zkEVM

Immutable zkEVM provides Ethereum-level security with zero gas fees for players. It's a Layer 2 zk-rollup, meaning all transactions are cryptographically proven and settled on Ethereum mainnet. Combined with Immutable Passport for wallet abstraction, players get the security of decentralized ownership without the friction of traditional crypto.
