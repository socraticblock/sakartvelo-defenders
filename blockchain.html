Player Experience

The Invisible Blockchain

Most blockchain games lead with crypto. We don't. The blockchain is silent infrastructure that players never interact with directly. Here's what the experience actually looks like.

✉

Log In With Email

Players sign up with email, Google, or Apple. Immutable Passport automatically creates and manages a wallet behind the scenes. No seed phrases, no browser extensions, no crypto tutorials. You click "Sign Up" and you're in.

★

Earn Through Gameplay

Earn a star for the first time and SAKART tokens appear in your balance. No mining. No staking. No daily quests to grind. You play the game well, the game rewards you. 600 total stars across 200 levels.

⚙

Spend on Cosmetics

Use SAKART to buy cosmetic tower skins and hero visual effects. Gameplay-affecting items are never sold — only earned. The marketplace lets players trade skins with each other, with a 1.8% fee sustaining development.

Token Details

SAKART Tokenomics

SAKART is a utility token with a fixed hard cap of 1 billion. Zero pre-mine. Zero team allocation. All tokens enter circulation exclusively through player gameplay.

Supply & Distribution

  • Hard Cap: 1,000,000,000 SAKART (fixed)
  • Pre-mine: Zero
  • Team allocation: Zero
  • Campaign rewards: ~700M (70% of cap)
  • Reserved: ~300M for seasons, competitive prizes, liquidity
  • Emission: First-time star unlocks only
  • Grinding: Replaying levels earns zero tokens
  • Purchase: SAKART cannot be bought from the game

Design Principles

  • No pay-to-earn: Spending money gives zero gameplay advantage
  • No grinding: Each star can only earn tokens once
  • Skill-gated: Better play = more tokens, period
  • Fixed supply: No inflation, no new tokens beyond the cap
  • Deflationary: Tokens spent on fees are burned
  • Circulation: Tokens move between players via marketplace
  • Separate economies: Gameplay resources and SAKART are independent

Token Emission

Earning SAKART

There is exactly one way to earn SAKART tokens: demonstrate skill for the first time. No exceptions. No loopholes. No grind.

★

Star-Based Earning

Each of the 200 levels offers 3 stars (600 total). When you earn a star for the first time, you receive SAKART tokens. Rewards scale with era — later eras grant more. The key word is first time — replaying earns nothing.

⚔

Era Scaling

Early era stars grant smaller rewards. Later era stars grant progressively more, reflecting both increased difficulty and the economic goal of distributing tokens over the long term rather than front-loading rewards.

▲

Bounded Supply

Total SAKART in circulation is directly proportional to active players and their campaign progress. A player earning all 600 stars accumulates a substantial but finite amount. No mechanism exists for earning beyond first-time completions.

The Anti-Grind Guarantee

Every token reward in Sakartvelo Defenders is one-time-only. You cannot replay content for tokens. You cannot pay to earn. You cannot bot your way to wealth. SAKART enters circulation only when a player achieves something they haven't achieved before. Replay farming is mathematically impossible.

Token Burns

Deflationary by Design

Multiple burn mechanisms create sustained deflationary pressure. Target: 2-5% annual deflation of circulating supply — enough to maintain token health without encouraging hoarding.

💰

Marketplace Fees

Partial burn on marketplace transactions. Every skin trade removes SAKART from circulation, creating continuous deflationary pressure proportional to player activity.

🔒

Skin Bind/Unbind

Equipping a skin binds it to a tower instance. Unbinding costs SAKART — which is burned. Common skins have minimal fees; rare and legendary skins have higher fees reflecting their marketplace value.

🏆

Seasons & Cosmetics

Season pass purchases, exclusive cosmetic effect unlocks, and tournament entry fees all consume SAKART. Each season introduces new burn mechanisms alongside new content.

Anti-Inflation Architecture

Five structural safeguards: capped earning per player, no replay farming, fixed 1B hard cap, continuous burn mechanisms, and completely separate gameplay economy. Token inflation cannot affect gameplay balance because the two economies never intersect.

Technical Architecture

Smart Contract Architecture

Three contracts. One chain. Zero gas fees. Built on Immutable zkEVM for Ethereum-grade security with a player-friendly experience.

◆

SAKART Token (ERC-20)

Fixed 1B supply with no minting function. Gasless transfers via Immutable account abstraction. Includes blacklist function for fraud prevention and multi-account detection, governed by multisig wallet.

ERC-20 1B Fixed Gasless
◆

Skin Contract (ERC-1155)

Supports both fungible (common skins, multiple copies) and non-fungible (rare/legendary, limited edition). Metadata on IPFS for censorship-resistant asset storage. Era-specific skins with distinct visual styles.

ERC-1155 IPFS Metadata Cosmetic Only
◆

Mastery Scroll (ERC-721)

Each scroll is a distinct non-fungible asset tracking type, stack count, and activation status. Verifies ownership before applying bonuses. Manages bind/unbind with automatic SAKART burn on unbind.

ERC-721 3-Stack Max Tradeable

Skins & Marketplace

True Ownership. Transparent Trading.

Cosmetic skins change tower and hero visuals without affecting gameplay. Implemented as ERC-1155 tokens for frictionless trading. Every marketplace item shows exactly what it does.

🎨

Skin Binding

When equipped, a skin binds to a specific tower instance. Bound skins cannot be traded until unbound, which costs SAKART (burned). Common skins have minimal fees; rare and legendary skins have higher fees reflecting marketplace value.

This creates a SAKART token sink while preventing rapid marketplace speculation on actively-used skins.

🔎

Marketplace Tooltips

Every marketplace item displays a clear tooltip showing: what the item does, which era/level it applies to, current bind status, and cost to unbind if applicable.

For mastery scrolls, the tooltip shows the full stack progression: +3% (1 copy), +6% (2 copies), +9% (3 copies max). Complete transparency — no external resources needed.

💰

1.8% Fee

1.3% to development treasury. 0.5% auto-compounded to marketplace liquidity pool. No hidden costs. One rate for everyone.

🔒

True Ownership

All cosmetic skins are NFTs on Immutable zkEVM. No centralized server can revoke them. Trade, sell, or hold — your skins, your choice.

⚖

Zero Pay-to-Win

Marketplace items are cosmetic only. No tower upgrades, no hero boosts, no gameplay advantages. A player with zero skins has the exact same power as one with a full collection.

Resilience

Never Interrupted. Never Offline.

The game works regardless of blockchain status. Full PWA architecture with offline support and automatic sync.

⚡

Chain Outage Fallback

If Immutable zkEVM experiences downtime, gameplay continues uninterrupted. All gameplay state — level progress, star completions, hero experience — is tracked locally and synced when connectivity resumes.

Blockchain transactions (marketplace trades, token transfers, NFT operations) are queued locally and submitted automatically in order when the chain comes back online. Players never lose progress due to infrastructure issues.

🔒

Wallet Integration

Immutable Passport handles wallet creation and management. New players create a wallet through simple social login (email, Google, Apple) without understanding blockchain concepts.

Experienced Web3 users can connect existing wallets via MetaMask or WalletConnect. Wallet connection is optional and deferrable indefinitely — the full 200-level campaign plays identically without one.

Security

Multi-Account Prevention

Without prevention, one person could farm SAKART across multiple accounts. Multiple layers ensure economic integrity.

👤

Identity Layer

Passport ties each account to a verified identity (email, phone, social login). Creating large numbers of accounts is difficult by design.

⏱

Time Investment

Progression requires completing 15/20 levels per era. Farming accounts must invest significant time before earning meaningful SAKART, making mass creation impractical.

🔎

Pattern Monitoring

The marketplace monitors for farming patterns — multiple accounts sending tokens to a single address within short timeframes. Suspicious activity flagged for investigation. Conservative thresholds prevent false positives (shared IPs, family members).

Fee Structure

No hidden fees. No premium tiers. No surprise costs. Here is every fee in the game, in one place, forever.

  • Tournament entry: SAKART fee (funds prize pool)
  • Gas fees: $0.00 — Immutable covers this

Development Revenue

Treasury safeguards: Monthly withdrawal cap. 48-hour timelock on all withdrawals. Fully auditable on-chain. Transparent by design.
