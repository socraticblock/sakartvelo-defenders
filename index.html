Core Design Principles

Built Different. Built Right.

⚔

Play First, Blockchain Second

The blockchain is invisible infrastructure. Players never need to understand crypto, wallets, or gas fees. They click "Play" and earn tokens naturally through skilled gameplay. No MetaMask tutorials. No seed phrases. Just play.

★

Skill > Wallet

Every gameplay-affecting item is earned through skill. A player who spends $0 has the exact same experience as someone who spends $1,000. SAKART tokens are earned by three-starring levels, not by swiping a credit card.

☠

History Drives Gameplay

This isn't "culture as skin." Tower types, enemy formations, hero abilities, and map designs are all drawn from real Georgian history. You're not just playing a tower defense -- you're defending actual historical battles.

Nine Eras of Georgian History

3,000 Years. One Epic Journey.

Each era introduces unique towers, enemies, heroes, and mechanics. Eras are unlocked sequentially -- you cannot skip ahead. Launch scope: Eras 0-2 (60 levels).

Era 0 / Tutorial

Ancient Colchis

~1500 BC - 500 BC

The legendary kingdom of the Golden Fleece. Dense forests, ancient stone ruins, the Black Sea coast. New player onboarding with simpler mechanics.

Era 1

Unified Kartli -- Kingdom of Iberia

~300 BC - 300 AD

Early Georgian kingdoms unifying against Roman and Parthian influence. Introduces hero abilities and synergy mechanics.

Era 2

Golden Age of Svaneti

~800 - 1200 AD

Svan tower-houses defending mountain passes against Arab raiders and Seljuk invaders. Advanced synergy chains in snow-capped fortresses.

Era 3

Renaissance Imereti

~1200 - 1500 AD

Bagrationi dynasty cultural renaissance. Mongol hordes, Timurid war elephants. Introduces mastery scroll system.

Era 4

Kakheti Wine Country

~1500 - 1700 AD

Wine, trade routes, and defense against Persian and Ottoman incursions. Flammable wine traps and gunpowder emplacements. Introduces PvP.

Era 5

King Tamar's Reign

~1180 - 1220 AD

The zenith of Georgian power. Royal guard towers, cathedral cannons, battlefield engineering. Most complex synergy combinations.

Era 6

Russian Annexation

~1800 - 1918 AD

The long occupation. Guerrilla defense towers, sabotage traps, resistance hideouts. Introduces survival mode.

Era 7

First Republic

~1918 - 1921 AD

Brief independence between empires. Shortest era. Propaganda towers, diplomatic embassies. Final PvP tier.

Era 8

Modern Georgia

~1991 - Present

Rose Revolution, the 2008 war, and modern identity. Hybrid warfare, cyber attacks, economic pressure as enemy types. Endgame content.

Core Game Loop

How It Works

Build Mazes

Place walls to create winding paths. Position towers for overlapping kill zones. Every wall segment and tower position matters -- a well-designed maze turns basic towers into a devastating defense.

Command Heroes

Directly control heroes from Georgian history and legend. Medea's toxic clouds, Queen Tamar's global damage boost, David the Builder's instant tower upgrades. Each hero has 2-3 active abilities with cooldowns.

Earn Stars

1-3 stars per level based on performance. Stars are tracked per-difficulty (Normal, Hard, Elite). First-time star unlocks earn SAKART tokens. Replays earn zero -- no grinding, no farming.

Unlock Eras

Three-star enough levels and pay a SAKART fee to unlock the next era. Each era brings new tower types, enemies, heroes, maps, and mechanics. The journey through Georgian history continues.
