Build. Defend. Command.

Master the Battlefield

Maze-building tower defense meets real-time hero command. Every wall matters, every tower synergizes, every hero ability turns the tide. Here's how it works.

The Core Loop

Four Phases. Every Level.

Each level follows a rhythm that deepens with complexity. The loop stays constant — the strategy never stops evolving.

⛪

Build

Place walls to shape enemy paths into mazes. Position towers behind walls for maximum coverage. Terrain — rivers, cliffs, ruins — constrains your layout. Every placement is a commitment.

⚔

Defend

Waves crash against your maze. Towers auto-attack based on type, range, and targeting priority. Enemies follow the path you built — or breach your walls if you left a gap. Adapt in real-time.

★

Command

Your hero isn't a statue — you command them directly. Activate abilities on cooldown: area damage, buffs, repositioning, emergency heals. Timing a hero ability at the right moment saves runs.

☆

Earn

Complete levels to earn gold, stars, and SAKART tokens. Stars unlock the next era. Mastery scrolls unlock harder modes. No pay-to-win — every reward comes from skill, not wallet size.

Enemy Forces

Know Thy Enemy

Enemy composition shifts every era. From ancient tribal raiders to Mongol siege engines to Soviet armor — each wave demands a different defensive approach.

⚔

Infantry Swarms

Fast, numerous, individually weak. Ancient tribal warriors, Arab raiders, Ottoman janissaries. They overwhelm through sheer numbers. Splash towers and slow fields are the answer.

☠

Armored Units

Slow, tough, resistant to basic damage. Mongol heavy cavalry, Timurid war elephants, Persian siege towers. Require focused fire from upgraded Basic towers or Special abilities to crack.

☢

Elites & Bosses

Era-ending boss enemies with unique mechanics. Persian Immortals that revive once. Mongol Khans that buff nearby units. Boss levels demand hero abilities and maze optimization.

⚡

Speed Runners

Scouts, cavalry, and raiders that move at double speed with low HP. They race through mazes before towers can cycle. Slow towers and hero area attacks are critical to stop them.

☤

Healers & Buffers

Shamans, priests, and medics that heal or shield nearby enemies. Prioritize them with manual targeting — if a healer reaches the middle of a swarm, the wave stalls and leaks.

♕

Siege Engines

Battering rams, catapults, and cannon that directly attack your walls and towers. They don't follow the path — they destroy it. Forces you to defend the maze itself, not just the endpoint.

Progression

Skill Opens Every Door

No loot boxes. No paywalls. Progression is purely skill-based with transparent requirements for every unlock.

★

Star Rating

Earn 1 to 3 stars per level based on performance: lives remaining, gold efficiency, and clear time. Three-starring a level is the core skill challenge. Stars are tracked per difficulty tier — Normal 3-stars are separate from Hard 3-stars.

🔒

Era Unlocking

To unlock the next era, you must 3-star all levels in the current era on Normal difficulty and pay a SAKART token fee. The fee prevents idle unlocking — you must be both skilled and economically engaged. No shortcuts.

📜

Mastery Scrolls

Mastery scrolls unlock harder modes and modifiers. Earned by completing optional challenge objectives — "no tower sells," "hero only," "maze under 40 walls." Each scroll is a trophy of genuine mastery, not grind time.

Difficulty Tiers

Three Ways to Suffer

Every level can be played on three difficulties. Same map, same towers — exponentially harder. Higher difficulties earn more SAKART and unlock exclusive rewards.

☆

Baseline

The intended experience. Standard enemy HP, normal wave pacing, generous gold economy. Completing Normal 3-stars unlocks the next era. Where you learn the map and develop your maze.

⚔

PvP Tournament Brackets

Available from Era 4+. Compete in tournament brackets where you design a maze and an attacker sends waves through it — then swap. Best-of-three. Winner earns SAKART and tournament ranking. No pay-to-enter, no advantage for spenders.

☠

Survival Mode

Available from Era 6+. Endless waves with escalating difficulty. No star rewards — just a pure leaderboard chase. How long can your maze hold? Survival mode features unique enemies not found in the campaign and weekly global leaderboards.
